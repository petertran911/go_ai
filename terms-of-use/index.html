    App’s Services

    The App consists in offering Users the possibility to generate and/or restyle images, with private and non-commercial use, based on the description of its content, either by indicating a written prompt or by uploading an already existing image (hereinafter, “the Services”).

    The App offers functionalities such as (by way of example, but not limited to):

    - Creating an AI generated image by indicating a written prompt.

    - The User can define the “style” of the resulting AI generated image by selecting a sample image from the catalogue of the App.

    - Exporting the resulting AI generated images.

    - Accessing the User history of previously used prompts and generated images.
